About us

Mary's Sweets was created from a deep believe that cakes and Sweets can be beautiful and taste really good too. We want to share the deliciousness of our Sweets made from organic ingredients, decorated with Mother Nature's colourings.

Established in Galway, we're a small family business, dedicated to to deliver our delicious creations anywhere in Ireland, in time to help you celebrate and make those special moments in life even more special. We invite you to have a look at the website and also visit us on our Social Media channels where you can see daily posts of our latest yummy creations and check the feedback left by our customers. Feel free to get in touch with us for all your questions, we're always happy to chat and assist in making your baking dreams came true.

If you've already made up your mind on what you want to get, then please use the form on this website to place your order. Thanks for your support!
